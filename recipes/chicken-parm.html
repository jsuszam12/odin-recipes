Lasagna Recipe

This is an easy chicken Parm recipe that's delicious. You can do this in an oven or a toaster oven.

Chicekn Parm is a very easy and delicious meal!

Here is how to make it!

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • Chicken
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat over
  2. Bake chicken for 20 minutes. Flipping chicken after 12 minutes.
  3. Spread cheese on top and bake until melted!
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
