Tacos Recipe

This is an tacos recipe!

Tacos are a very easy meal and easy to make!

Here is how to make it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tortillas
  • Onion and cilantro
  • Salsa verde or Roja.

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Dice onion and cilantro
  3. Heat tortillas and add the meat to the tortillas.
  4. Add lime and salt to taste, and your favorite sauce!
